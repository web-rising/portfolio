Our mission.

WebRising seeks to help small businesses navigate the advent of web-based platforms while empowering volunteers with real-world experience and a greater sense of community.

Who we are.

We are a non-profit organization connecting volunteer high school developers to small businesses in need of a web-based platform to market their business, completely free of charge.

What we offer.

Whether you require a static website to simply showcase your business and display important information, or a platform to expedite sales — we've got you covered.
